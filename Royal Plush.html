Royal Plush

Game Overview

Royal Plush follows the story of a plush teddy bear in an ordinary, yet mysterious household. Gain powerful abilities as you do quests for your friend, King Snake. Swallow items and bounce all over the place. Climb effortlessly with octopus tentacles. Glide and dive to traverse the house at ultra high speeds with bat wings. Sneak around to avoid the mom, baby and roomba as they mutate into horrific forms. Escape before it's too late!​​​​​​​

Genre: Multiplayer Party Game

Platform: PC

Role: Programmer and Level Designer

Game Tools: Unity and Maya

Initial Concept

The original concept for the game had our team think of a concept that we would all enjoy making. This lead to the idea of a toy coming to life as you traverse the house while you interact with many household items. The game would then shift tone due to become more macabre and scary as you progress with the game

Contributions

My role for this project was the game's Level Designer. Truthfully this was my first time making a game purely as a level designer thus I learned some new skills in the process. I designed the main house the player was going to explore through. Starting with the layout of the house, I took to making it look like an old house from the 1950s. ​​​​​​​
